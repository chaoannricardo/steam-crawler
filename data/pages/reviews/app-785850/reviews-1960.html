As a game less than 10 USD and less than 1G's capacity, I think it's not bad.

And there are few things that I think the Dev can improve in the future or some tips for new players:
1.The mission has 4 types, which are Main Quest, Side Quest and Missions(Errand and Hunt). In these types, only Errand cannot control by the player, so be careful when you pick up the mission, or you may end up to wait all your parties to finish the mission, or choose to cancel the mission to call them back.
2.If you didn't select the skills before you promote your students, then you won't have chance to select the skills again, so remeber to choose the skills BEFORE you promote your students.
3.The diffculties of the different level's missions is quite big, so be aware of the party member you choose before you start the mission, cause no matter you fail or not, the time in the game still goes on.
4.Some Weapons or Equipments status is not the same as it shows on the student's menu. Ex: Kujang's ATK is 99 but things quite different when in battle, and after the battle you will find out that the student's status is different with student's menu. This should wait the Dev to fix.
5.Right now there is only 10(1+3+6) class in the game, hope there will be more in the future.
6.You can change your student's name by using "Nickname"

At the end I have to say:Although the game still needs to be improve, but it's worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
