-This game is a bit unbalance . this game is expecting you have a very good learn curve because the AI is already strong from start . You can easily lose first few missions .

-Five buildings/Five Lanes is not enough? The main of this game is to use your pets to destory 3 of the building on the opposite side .You got 6 types of main building in 5 lanes , in early game you dont have much choice usually 2 dojo, 2 bells and 1 other .You couldnt really play with your style like (1 dojo 2 school + others ) because this game is too focus on units ,strong magic is good but without a unit you cant even win the game. You dont have choice and have to go for 3 dojo because oppoents is doing 3 dojo as well .

-first units of production of the Dojo is random so you cant control your first 2-3 pet type be , you lose your first 3 moves , you lose the game.

- Theme is not suitable ? There are not much difference between Ninja cats or Samurai dogs.They have same classes so they have same skills.Even the start place of the story are the same. so you can really change cats to fishes , dogs to bears .
