\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThough the price is alright but the gameplay is really repetitive and basic. To me, being difficult is one thing, but it's not at all challenging as you are just spending all the time waiting to stab those tiny weak spots while they are totally unable to hit you as well. And after finishing the training section and started the navice difficulty, I already feel repetitive as you realize the gameplay isn't gonna go anywhere.

I get the point that the dev wants to focus on swordfighting itself and not gameplay progression but hitting is not satisfying either since the in-game physic is not responsive. You don't feel like hitting the opponent, nor do you feel like you are reflecting their attacks (maybe the vibration setting is too weak). Sound effect is weak too. When you cut the enemy there's only the low-quality bloodspilling sound.

Really really want to like the game and was waiting for it since the trailer. It just turned out to be another tech demo that's not polish enough to sell (at any price).\t\t\t\t\t
