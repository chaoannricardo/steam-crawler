\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReally good game, looking forwards if the developer will release 'this is the police 3'

Dark and murky mood throughout the game, but definitely should try it

A sad but meaning story, the twisted ending really surprise me. However I think it is a bit short compared to 'this is the police' and the difficulties for management is much more easier than the first game

The combat system looks real nice but need improvement such as able to fast forward actions( it is sometimes really boring to look at characters moving slowly) and moving blocks be clearer. Also, in my opinion, perks can be designed better. For me, 90% you can use the same perks with steath approach to clear the sitaution without any cops injuryed. Due to the rather short story line, the chance you play the combat system is not much. Hope there will be further updates to add more assaults and make full use of the combat system.

I think one of the special parts for 'this is the police' is their investigations where you can break down gangs and criminals. For this game, interrogations and torture are added for players to receive useful information to break down gangs which is nice. However, when I speak gangs, I litterally mean 2 gangs only which is far less than the first game.

Overall, if you are a fan who love 'this is the police' series, you undoubtedly should purchase this game. Although I think overall the first game is better, this isn't a worthless sequel. The story telling and the helpless mood built up in first game is still here. The soundtrack is still really nice and worth listening to. It may not live up to the expectation of the first game but the new elements added are a breath of fresh air to this game.

My rating for this game: 7.5/10\t\t\t\t\t
