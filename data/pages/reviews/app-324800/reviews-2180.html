Review in English
Good:
1. Make Shadow Warrior Great Again.
2. Succeed most of success ideas from Shadown Warrior 1.
3. By now, you can hard kill most of bosses by your firepower.
4. Great graphics, great details.
4A. Weathers: Sunny, rainy, thunderstorm, snow, night, day.
4B. Many destructible and explosive items
4C. Scenery: Japanese town, wild land, hilly farm, canyon, cyberpunk metropolis.
4D. Many monsters types, demons, demonized animals, robots and drones, human gangsters; all of them are detailed even after you take them down.
5. Great sound and music that never obsolete.
6. Side missions extend gaming hours.("ancient Chinese secrets" is NOT an easy achievement)
6A. Well hided secrets.
7. Many weapons, many upgrades, many skills.
7A. You will need restart the game for more upgrades.
7B. By those skills, you are REAL shadow warrior now and make Duke Nukem like an idiot.
7C. Elemental damage buff/immune/resist like RPG games.
7D. Free switch weapon wheel.
8. No more damage from drop.(except the abyss....it's everywhere in the game.)
9. Fast pace of battles like Doom, and enjoy the fun like Doomguy.
10. Easter eggs like ZL 209(ED 209 from Robocop) are still fun.
11. Their own 3D engine, not EGS Unreal engine.

Bad:
1. Random crashes after hours played.
2. 9 kind of ammo and over 2 dozen of weapons, but you could only have 8 of them on the weapon wheel.
2A. Sometimes, you have to open the weapon wheel for change weapon setup and may cut your fun in battle.
3. Fixed mod maps, when you have played missions multiple times, you will remember everything.
3A. Some maps are not full sealed the edge, you may fall into limbo, considered as abyss.
3B. In game map is available, but only reveals your surround areas and NOT support for elevated map.(Doom have far better map than Shadow Warrior)
3C. Sometimes, you will be stop by the edge of map with an invisible wall.
4. Item management interface is bad when you have load of gems.
4A. When you buy or sell, just one by one and each buy/sell action takes 2 seconds.
5. You can forget the story in the game.
5A. Wang's personality describe is still bad, just talking with load of harsh languages.
6. Those Polishes had not used Kenji or Mandarin words correctly.
