\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAmnesia: The Dark Descent is a first-person puzzle game with horror elements. Recommend for those who love puzzle games and horror games.

Pros:
+Great story, keep me going to know what's up.
+Good atmosphere and detailed level design.
+Intuitive controls with mouse: you can grab boxes, throw stones, etc.
+Puzzles work like what you think in the real world!

I don't think there are something bad about this game, but I will list some of my thoughts here:
You probably only want to play this once. After you know the story and the puzzles, there is just no reason to replay it.(or maybe yes if you want to experience different endings)
The mechanism of the enemy is quite simple and predictive. If you know how to deal with them, they won't post much threat.

9/10\t\t\t\t\t
