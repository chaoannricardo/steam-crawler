Currently my play time is 2 hours. Achievement is 9/13.
I expect I need 2 more hours to get all achievement.
--
I eventually got all achievements in 5 hours.
It is because I felt asleep while I was playing which cost me 1 additional hour for the sleeping playtime.
--
Two Digits is an interesting math game.
The objective is to find two groups of number from 9 provided numbers
such that the sum of the two group is the same.
(You don't need to use all the 9 numbers but one achievement is to use all the 9.)

Pros:
1. BGM is good but a little bit too short.
2. Practice your brain.

Cons:
1. The scroll of the mouse is not supported. (Non convenient in the menu page)
2. After fulfill the achievements, I need to close the game and open it again to make it appear.

Small trick
Although the game requires you to obtain two groups of numbers with the same sum,
it will be helpful to calculate the difference of two numbers with a small value.\t\t\t\t\t
