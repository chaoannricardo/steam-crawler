\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy rating: 6.5/10

Good:
1. As PC itself was based on Panzer General; this DLC was based on Allied General.
2. The ONLY campaign avaliable for UK in PC.
(in other campaigns or DLCs, UK roles as TARGET dummy and no more campaign playable.)


Bad:
1. Over loyalty with Allied General, only few surprises during the campaign.
2. Campaign tree is TOO SIMPLE, just following the history and only 1 chance(Market Garden) to change it.
3. Low units cap as Allied General, even at final battle, you may only have 28 units on the field.
4. Soviet corps is better than Allied Corps.
5. West Allies have to fight with inferior ground units.
6. POWERFUL Italian army and navy. Italian army got more armor units than history, better motorized for slowly units, high experience units are all around.
6A. Also, Regina Marina challange against RN and USN at Sicily; HOW DARE THEY ARE after I'd finished TRIUMPH at Taronto raid.(sink 6 Italian battleships for triumph; in history, RN had stroke down 4 Italian battleships, aftter that raid, Adm. Yamamoto have got the ideas from UK and made the plan for attack at Pearl Harbor)


Tips:
1. Artilleries are important to protect your infantries and tanks.(but not ensure the survivability for fragile UK/US tanks)
1A. US have better artillery than UK. M12 GMC is the best SPG in the game. 8in > 7.2in, M7 Preist > 25pdr Sexton. Sherman 105>Cornwell CS.
2. AIR FORCE, AIR FORCE, AIR FORCE. But beware AA guns.
2A. UK have best tactical bombers in the game.(Mosquito IV S2) US B26 and A26 are not as powerful as before.(in Panzer General series)
2B. Remember your unit cap, airforce(excluded SE units) should below 1/3 of your deployed units or you will have insufficent ground units, and tanks need artillery covers.
2C. Try to have more Spitfire SE fighters, they can be upgrade to Meteor at OP: Cobra, best Allied fighter with JET POWER and huge fuel tank!!!
2D. At deployment phase, there should only have 10-11 air units avaliable to deploy. Make a plan for training your air units.
3. More mines than Soviet campaign, you need mine sweepers.
4. Before Sherman Firefly avaliable, have some M10s to deal with German tanks.
4A. Even III L or IV H could kill Shermans easily.
4B. Tigers and Panthers are crashing US/UK tanks like bugs.
4C. Churchill tanks have better survivability BUT they are hard to harm Tigers and Panthers.
4D. M26 avabiable at battle of Crossing the Rhine.
4E. If you have triumph at Market Garden than liberate Netherland, Sherman Firefly is your BEST TANK in Allied Campaign.(If you want to change history, play UK and have more elite UK tanks)
4F. Try to lure Tigers and Panthers run into towns or rough terrain, than send infantry units to kill them.
