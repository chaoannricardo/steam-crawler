\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhite noise2 High-end version of Hide&seek, worthy of sharing with friends

Perhaps you have critical courage to judgment and the ability to manipulate the role accurately;
Perhaps you like to have only a beam of light, alone in the dark ruins
Maybe you love to use all kinds of abilities, step by step, follow your goals, and enjoy the fun of chasing.
Then try this game.

White noise 2(after the text is called White Noise) is an multiplayer indie horror game ,use the first person from the perspective (whether the investigator or monster), developed and published by Milkstone Studios, released last April, support PC\u3001PS4\u3001Xbox one, can be purchased at steam.


Specifically, the game is divided into two camps: the investigator, the monster (any camp will play by AI if there is no human player). In each round of the maps, 8 different clues are randomly appearing for the investigator (1~4) to search with a compass, At this time ,the monster needs to summon idols, obstruct the discovery of clues, and chase the seeker , trying to swallow it.

Victory conditions:
Monster: need to devour all the explorers.
investigator: take full advantage of the character's characteristics with the selected flashlight, (each team will not have duplicate roles).
Find 8 Clues, open artifact, seal with the number of the correspond player\u2019s sigil.
After being devoured, the investigator can help his teammates in a spiritual form without being completely destroyed.


Ps: Explorers hinder the monster's main medium: lights. Light can slow the monster's movement speed, but every time the light shines, the monster's next appearance, the anti-light increases.

In short, when you play the white noise, each player can select different numerical characteristics of the investigator /monster, through a variety of ways, do brain competition, It is a well-balanced multiplayer cooperative game.

The following:

I personally prefer this kind of escape edition game than confrontation of the system.
About the white noise game painting style, is belongs to oil painting, partial realism.
The game investigator is eclectic, with characters from all over the world to choose from, lovely South Korean Yumi, giant milk petite Rinko, steady Russian agent Nikolai, American black antiques dealer Phileas, Chinese e-sports player Jin (also a set of Bruce Lee dress up) ...Each has its own dedicated voice.

In the case of monster, face score is difficult to say, but Darcy(favorite), Lilith raise the average level.
additional, investigator will experience extraordinary if they have a incompetent teammate
