\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played this game a long time ago and don't even bother to rewrite a memory-blurried comment again just for another steam sale.

Antichamber was one of the earliest games that drew me into this DRM collection nightmare (yes I joined Steam quite late until it became essential for some games), I bought it with full price and enjoyed the wonderful 3 days. In advance I heard and knew it could be mindfucxed but still walked myself through it smoothly because I truly love that kind of alien logic and outerplanet creativity. Plus a map/visual cliche I discovered on my old computer (not sure I can duplicate that now) helped me a lot to complete all the signs on wall without totally getting lost: when you see through a certain piece of glass from the other side, you may find the map on wall magically filled by all (hidden ones included) chambers. Legal cheating, I know ;D That was how I beat the great hard game in 3 days. The Ending is rather impressive once you collect all the signs, whilst the whole game plot is your life story.

I will return to this game again when I entirely forget all the things within.\t\t\t\t\t
