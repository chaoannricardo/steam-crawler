I found the protagonist a bit slow on realizing the resentments that his 2 female companies have on him. In my opinion, the story of this VN is not as moving as the previous sakura VNs, but it gives a more realistic feeling to the readers on the fact that this kind of story can happen to anyone as well as its well-designated graphics.

Positive points
storyline, for it being realistic
characters
graphics
soundtracks
Negative points
storyline, for it being a little boring
protagonist to be reacting sometimes stupidly

It is not a fantastic visual novel, but I think it's still woth a try, might be useful to look it as an experience^^.\t\t\t\t\t
