This is actually one of the best Call of Duty Campaign since the first modern warfare, it's quite a surprise since I bought it mainly for the MW remaster, and was quite \u2665\u2665\u2665\u2665\u2665\u2665 off about the who bundle thing, but the story and art design and gameplay just works really well, I like the choose your own mission design as well, so you can take on different side quests to break up the campaign so its not too monotonous, the characters can sometimes be a little over the top for my liking but hey this is not Shakespeare, I have no problem with the Sci-fi setting either since I'm huge SF nerd, the overall design and militarized spaceships just works, function and fashion, got no complain on that front either.

Overall 8 out 10 for me, it's a shame they won't explore this setting further with all the cries for \u201cBoots on the ground\u201d, if I want boots on the ground I'll just play battlefield or older COD games, I don't get all the complains about it, but well it's just me.\t\t\t\t\t
