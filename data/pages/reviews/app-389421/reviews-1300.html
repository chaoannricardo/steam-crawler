\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy rating:
8.5/10

Good:
1. As historical Red Army, you would following the way from Great Purge to capture Berlin.
2. You may deploy many units in some battle.(Max: 45 units + SP units at Operation Bagration)
3. Most battlefields have backdoor for retreat.(See tips)
4. Many events: Like battle of Stalingard, the battlefield would getting smaller and smaller depend on turns switch; Retreat from Barbarossa German rush; force Romania to switch side....etc.
5. Triumph may trigger extra battles, fight those battles to get extra bonus.

Bad:
1. Painful and hard battles at early part of campaign.
2. In some battles, there would have more German units than yours.
3. KV1 and KV85 are DIFFERENT chassis, WTF a bug.
4. Never have a chance to change history. All battles are following the history.
5. Battle of Manchuria with poor design to increase the diffculty.
5A. Mountains around Manchuria?
5B. Only 16 units avaliable to deploy
5C. No backdoors.
5D. Wrong deployment for IJA units and wrong IJAAF air units.
6. Soviet infantries are inferior than German or Western Allies.


Tips:
1. Buy more units than deployment allowed, 45 for OP: Bagration.
2. USE BACKDOORS to switch your units or retreat low strength units.
3. Fire support from artilleries are crucial for infantries survival.
4. You need AA guns at early battles. Soviet early fighters VS Me109 are SUICIDE missions.
5. Watch out fuel for your aircrafts. Many Soviet aircrafts are flying with small tank, don't leave airports too far.
