My rating: 8/10

Like Mini Metro, but have higher pressure and more frustration in this game.

Good:
1. Trains run like models in displaying.
2. The towns growth after received train services.
3. Very really switch control.
4. Easy to start.
5. Fast pace without undo, save, load,
6. Make plans for campaign levels.
7. If you are new in transportation games, go practice in sandbox, don't try campaign deep.

Bad:
1. VERY easy to lose the game from BANKRUPT.
1A. No debt or bond avaliable, if your cash down to 0, you lost.(Try to have more than 5k cash at ANYTIME, it hard, I know.)
1B. Classic campaign levels starts with low cash and very easy run to 0.
2. Classic campaign is HARD. Not only for all objectives, but also for survival.
2A. The extra objectives are not easy, have to try many times.(with little luck)
2B. With low cash, only few tracks/lines avaliable, NEVER make mistakes; if you do something wrong, you lose.
2C. Crash means lose, you wouldn't have enough cash to repair or recovery from the crash losses.
2D. Sometimes, trains may crash from your reverse order.
3. Easy start game with unfriendly diffcult.
3A. No diffcult options avaliable.
3B. With only few helps, newbies may fall into confusion and frustration than abandon or boo this game.
4. Bridges and tunnels are fixed and we can't build new bridges or tunnels by our own.(but even I can build, no money to do such job)
5. If you want to be a game maker, try NOT make a HARD game with cult/lovely looks.
6. Game may crash when you starting.
7. Sometimes, trains explode ITSELF!!!!!
