A well-polished little indie game!
With cute rendering art style and background music that varies according to the area you are in and your current action.

There's nothing really special in the game mechanics,
but every decision and implementation is executed perfectly and it really makes the game stand out!

Players can freely wander and explore the island and at the same enjoy the wonderful view.
Encountering different villagers, helping them find their lost items, playing games with them, or racing with them.
Collecting golden feathers so you can climb up higher mountains and find more hidden treasures or secret spots.

Overall, the dynamic music and the 3d pixelated art style really stand out.
Besides that, the level design implicitly leads the player to different events and areas.
And, the game is really cheap!
