I will only recommend this as long as something better doesn't come along.
It's a pretty good public transport simulator, and it is, in no way, shape or form, a simcity clone. Cities: Skylines is for that. However, I get 7 buses randomly stuck together in the middle of a highway very often once I have built a relatively large network, so I really can't fully recommend it, but it is the only simulator for *city public transport* (openttd, train/transport fever are more about intercity transport or transport of goods) other than CiM1, which I personally do not really like. It is pretty limited though, and why the heck can I not add letters for my line numbers? why is everything numbered anyway? I do see the potential but it's not really fulfilling it, kind of like SimCity 2013.

Side note, Cities: Skylines is a pretty good public transport simulator, but I don't feel like building up a city just to manage public transport.\t\t\t\t\t
