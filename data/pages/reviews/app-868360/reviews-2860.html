\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a great hospital simulation game, which is more realistic when compared with what we have previously.
Vintage graphics, complete hospital management experience.
You can definitely feel the joy when you have your patients all cured and happy.

But there are still so much that the game can improve even further, and I think some of them are quite fundamental for a management simulation game.

1. There should be a progress bar or progress % of the treatments.
2. The status description of the patients should be more detailed.
e.g. What is causing the pause of the treatments? What is the patient waiting? What is the status of the hospitalization?
3. Schedule of staff should be available for managing, especially for doctors.
4. Priority system should be implemented for us to micromanage the priority of treatments, especially for surgical department.
5. More extensive types of management should be added, e.g. pricing, marketing, etc.

Still, this is a wonderful game to try. I am still very into the game hours and hours after nearly 200 hours of gameplay.
Thanks for the effort of the company!\t\t\t\t\t
