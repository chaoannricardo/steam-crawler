I'm not good at construct efficient English but I'm trying to help some players if they wondering to buy this game.

The characters are delicate with drawing quality and that's the best part of this game.
However, this is a game that you can have it when you don't care about the following shortages:


1) There are flaws in this game, such as the some voice lines are not balanced.

2) Ending is very quick, sometimes you think there should be more in the following then it ceases suddenly. Good story but bad construction in terms of how to visualize them in the game.

3) The little game during story is repeated not only the same way, somehow a little bit boring. I can tell the creator want to let players have more fun during reading stories, but the little game quality wasn't that good.
