Lunnye Devitsy
[6.5/10]

[English Version]
Lunnye Devitsy is a fantasy adventure style puzzle game where the aim is to find several different ways to land on the moon.

The concept of the game is quite simple; the screen is soft while music is comfortable. The only thing that would be challenged is the need of controlling precision. Some places really need precise control of the keyboard (such as climbing, jumping).

I feel like I was watching a fascinating fantasy story when playing this game. That's the reason why I'd recommend this game. Give a try if you love 2D adventure games. ^^
