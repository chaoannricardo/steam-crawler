Hero of the Kingdom II is a short but delicate puzzle-based adventure RPG game. You'll spend most of the time collecting objects, trading and helping people (solving quests), then complete the whole story within several hours (probably). The graphics is as smooth as the previous version; with nice background music and decent plot that are able to provide players comfortable and relaxing gaming experiences.

The difficulty is fair, not too easy but not impossibly hard. I personally spend 8 hours to get 100% achievements, and truly enjoy every moment figuring out the tricky puzzles and finding those damn eggs and mushrooms. (probably top 5 of my most-enjoyable-100% achievements games.)

The Improvement would be too-short game length and limited use of collections (you can either use them for quests need or sell them only). Also the plot is sort of monotonous, which means side quests are few, yet it is still very entertaining.

A neat and fun game that highly recommend to puzzle/indie game lovers and 100% achievements collectors. I'll definitely look forward to more future work.

ps. I bought this game with the 25% off price, but still feel worth it. :D
