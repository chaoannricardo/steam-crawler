\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA match-3 game which you can move tile among an entire row/column. However total 8 types of tiles make this game less fun.

Although it may seems player has a lot of options since you can move a tile to anywhere among the row/column, the fact is player has significantly fewer options than the typical match-3 game (you can only move tiles to the adjacent position, but only 5 types in total). Several times it happened that player left with only few bad choices.

I can't say it's the most enjoyable match-3 game since sometimes you need to rely completely on RNG spawns you good tiles, also broken-heart tiles is punishing for combo style.

6/10 overall, worth playing.\t\t\t\t\t
