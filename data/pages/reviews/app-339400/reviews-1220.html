\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRunestone Keeper (RK) is a rougelike game. Your objective is to go up, as far as possible.
RK is similar to the Android game Dungelot (Dun) although the system of RK is more complicated.
However, the tactics for the two games are different due to different in-game settings.

Comparing with Dun,

1. RK has more characters to unlocked.

2. RK has the god system. The Gods can buff you for some parameters and debuff you for others.

3. RK has more random events.

4. The items in RK is acitve while the items in Dun is passive+active which makes the difficulty of RK higher.

5. The map for each level in RK is much larger to increase the difficulty.

6. The parameters of the enemies in RK are much higher while the shields can recover in every level.

7. The levels of the characters is reset every game which also increases the difficulty.

...

In short, I like and recommand both games.\t\t\t\t\t
