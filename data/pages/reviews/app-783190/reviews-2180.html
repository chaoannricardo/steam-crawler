If you are looking for a not-so-hard nostalgic RPG, you don't need to look further. Even I might compare Sin Slayers with Darkest Dungeon, but from the aspect of torturing your heroes and your own mind, you can still consider this a liter RPG for you when you've got some time to beat some levels, farms some items.

Personally I don't like the controls with controller in the battle, you need to move the cursor to select a skill then move it again on enemies to confirm your action. I just don't like how it goes. XD But it goes well in the Church.
-

I accidentally compare Sin Slayer with Darkest Dungeon. Though I prefer art style of Darkest Dungeon, but the overall visual in Sin Slayers is still good in its style.
-

I haven't reached far enough in the game so I don't know if I can do it in the future. (The gear upgrading will be in progress when you advance in the game) However, my inventory is piling up for 1 or 2 pages of unused gears so it is better if we can dismantle unused gears.
