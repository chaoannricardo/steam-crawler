CONS: no more update. no more improvement.

Not much better than its EA stage. You can find other alt.game to play, unless you love aliens more.

-----------------------------------

Previous review:

Good game experience. it's my second survival game. the first one is THE FOREST.
I think it's quite different from THE FOREST although they use the same engine.
As It is released just two month, it's hard to compare with THE FOREST.
Please remind that VORTEX is an EARLY ACCESS game so its contents aren't as many as THE FOREST.

Pros:
1. horror at night.
2. beautiful planet.
3. Dragon. I love it.
4. many animals/monsters to kills.

Cons:
1. Bugs. eg. rib suddenly disappeared in inventory, broken trap but can't repair suddenly.
2. low-end computer can't play smoothly even I play with low graphic quality, frame rate is around 30 something. MY PC: i5 4430S, 8GB RAM, GT740M 4GB RAM
3. Can't sleep in the game? I can't find the way to sleep.
4. No tutorial. Maybe I didn't find it. (luckily, I have played THE FOREST before)

Advice:
1. make it much playable with low-end PC.
2. survival mode is quite difficult to play. hard to avoid the monster attack, not easy to sneak around even i stay in the shelter/trees/anything where I can hide.( maybe it's my problem...), I think developer should let player to set the difficulty like FALLOUT series. yeah, I know, I know, VORTEX is a survival game...but there are a lot of different players, such as me. I would like to feel the horror but I really don't want to be killed easily. yeah, I know, I'm not a survival game expert
3. SLEEP is an essential function for a survivor. please let me SLEEP. OR make a guide to teach me?
4. yeah, a guide or a tutorial.
5. please update it with more contents, eg. crafting, farming system, fishing...blablabla...=)

SCORE: 7.5/10\t\t\t\t\t
