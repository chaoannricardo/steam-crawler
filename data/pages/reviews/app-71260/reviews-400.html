\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe PC port seems to have a few rough edges. After a bit of trial and error, I workaround the gameplay/music unsync problem.
Some people may encounter even worse bugs.

Otherwise, this is still one of the best rhythm game out there.
It has nice beats going. It's cheerful and funny. Even the enemies are cute.

Eventhough it's just 4 direction and 2 buttons, it's actually really joyful to look at, because every stage is different and everyone has different (dance) moves.
While other rhythm games just switch background with same visual elements that goes along the game mechanic.

Space Channel 5 is beautiful. That's the word I can think of it.
