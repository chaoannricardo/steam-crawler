DBS is a very interesting math game

Pro
1. Suitable number of stages. There are 5 worlds, each containing 30 stages.
2. As you proceed, there are floors with different function. You won't feel repetitive.
3. The difficulty of clearing all stages (non 3-star) is low while the difficulty for all achievements (same as all 3-star) is challenging but not too difficult.
4. Dev mentioned that there may be an update around Christmas in the discussion board.
The most IMPORTANT point is
5. You can cut a sheep into two parts and stick them together later.

Cons:
1. The difficulty of the 5th world is lower than the 4th.
2. No windowed mode.
3. No steam cloud.
