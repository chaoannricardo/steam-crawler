\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly this game is quite good...... until stage 5 BOSS.
Too much bullet will CHANGE their direction WITHOUT any hint, makes them unpredictable.
The same problem appears in stage 3 BOSS, but in stage 3 you're given more space and time to get away from the "unpredictable range", while in stage 5 you can only seeing bullet change their directions to hit your face JUST when you
dodged them.

OK, I know they have patterns,
bullets change directions periodically and is not random, but it's almostly impossible to find the path WHILE playing. You can record playing and re-watch againg to find a right way to dodge... but I don't think this is a good design.

There are many ways to make game harder, this one is just not reasonable. It's a punishing design, not a challenging design.

On the other side, like I wrote on the top, this game is quite good.
I don't expect the Boss design will be patched (very deep in design, too much to fix and, well, maybe not really much people think it's an issue), but if developers make another STG in the future, I hope this issue will be considered, and I'll buy it again.\t\t\t\t\t
