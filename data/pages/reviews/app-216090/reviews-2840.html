\r\n\t\t\t\t\t[English]
Go Home Dinosaurs! is a TD game with a very cute style. In the game, players control a groundhog to collect coconuts (construction resources) and gold (upgrade resources) to stop dinosaurs to get their BBQ. The length is not long (60 waves in total), while difficulty is obviously lower than other TD games, but this makes it a relaxing game that is suitable for all ages.

I personally love the variety and cuteness of towers so much; since they are all "alive" and talking all the times, I felt like I'm really engaged with the defence while I was playing. In addition, soundtrack is nice and graphics are clear and delicate.

Give it a shot if you think you're not really into TD game but want to try it. You'll probably love this super-cute game. :)

Go Home Dinosaurs!
