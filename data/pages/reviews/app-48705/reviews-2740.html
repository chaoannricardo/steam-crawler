\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBest game DLC ever it has lots of different weapons factions all are what I will be listing below at a later date in this review... so the factions consist of austria Prussia United Kingdom or the British and France
... the weapons are sabres muskets axes and bayonets it's amazing in regiment vs regiment fighting you can command a regiment of up to 40 troops
The units are cannons calavary infantry skirmishers (form of infantry or scouts or if you want to interpret it diferently their snipers) there is no current campaign but there is a mod for it...
now back to weapons calavary can equip weapons consisting of sabres muskets or pistols they are used to either hold or help your infantry if they are over welmed with enemy's...
They are better for more experienced players who understand how strategy works and how their brain works in the straigtegic world of games... cannons.... three types the mortor howtzer and normal cannons... the cannon is used at a far distance but far less range then the howtzer and the mortor
What you want to do with the howtzer or mortor is fire at targets from a farther distance meaning you should get back away from all opposing forces when firing. The cannon has 2-3 shell types a normal shell the HE shell or high explosive shell and the canister. The canister is used on targets closing in wether their charging you with bayonets or the calavary is charging you. Now on to the infantry... the infantry is typical soldiers equipted with bayoneted musket. The muskets are very unaccurate and hard to fire at targets from far distances. When firing at close ranges it's best to fire a volley or fire at my command when firing you will want to let your men take aim for at least 3 seconds. After those 3 seconds the men will have picked their targets and will be able to get an effective volley on the enemy. Charging is with infantry. When charging you will want to get your enemy's volley off the. Fire yours so they'll be injured and low on morale once charging you will left click and hold it to full kill you hostile on impact and from the sharp blade slashing threw their gut head arm or legs
... it is best to do a charge with heavy infantry with low range weapons because they will most likely have a saber to fight with... if you only like swords I'm recommending you stick with warband... but another option is using cavalry dragoons
