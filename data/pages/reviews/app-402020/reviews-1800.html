So let's be clear now, this game, <The Park>, is a advertisement of <The Secret World>, it is like a sidestory of The Four Silences. It is good, because now you know her story(no spoiler!), and you know her purpose. When I say good, because I'm a TSW player, and I'm a big fan.
If you are not a TSW player, you have no interest in TSW, or you still can't complete The Four Silences(far away from Tokyo), DON'T BUY IT.
