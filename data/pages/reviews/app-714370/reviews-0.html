========================================

Imagine BIOHAZARD 2 RE, then you will see how to deal with these zombies (soliders with full gear)...

But why those zombies can throw rolling grenades just like the agent from DIVISION...

OK... the game reminded me... I'm only Human, NOT Snake....

------------------------

Theme story and graphic design is good but too ordinary for battle system and story line(no stunt action/no impressive moment/no QTE in cutscenes) , lack of burst/ passionate events ... etc. But not a major point... even Metal Gear, the greatest cutscenes is created for twice snake only, but every one of metal gear series is good (exclude SURVIVE)

Too many people suppose this is a shooting game or TPS, but no . So think about it before get this. And I recommend to select easy mode if you don't wanna take too much troubles for quick run.If you select standard, please imagine BIOHAZARD 2 RE, then you will see how to deal with these zombies

Anyway, it 's worth playing for stage design (later-stages), characters design and the world at war theme, but you might want to buy it when over 20~30% off\t\t\t\t\t
