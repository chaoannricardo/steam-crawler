\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros:
- Superb graphic rendering
- Stable connectivity during online matches
- Freedom of customisation of mobile suits
- High replayability with balanced parts price

Cons:

System-wise:
- Aiming system constantly switch targets on its own
- Lack of transforming capability for Transformable Mobile Suits
- Yuki (Final Boss) 's AI being set with ridiculously high attack power that can continuously stun and result in quick K.O.
- Relatively long reloading time for mid/long-range weapons

Misc:
- Room of improvement for plot script-writing
- Relatively small roster of usable Mobile Suits (subject to change in the future, given the promised roster expansion with DLCs)

Verdict:
This game is not exactly abysmal or outright trash, but could be improved and polished. As it is in its current state, the pricing is a bit on the steep side.\t\t\t\t\t
